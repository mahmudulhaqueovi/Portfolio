About Me

I am a Civil & Structural Engineer with a B.Sc. from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Dhaka. My passion lies in designing safe, efficient, and innovative structural systems.

Currently working as a Jr. Structural Engineer at Artesian Engineering and Consultant Ltd., I have hands-on experience with steel structures, reinforced concrete buildings, glass & aluminium frame systems, and Detailed Engineering Assessments (DEA).

My research focuses on seismic performance of high-rise buildings, shear wall optimization, and sustainable concrete materials — with publications in international journals including Wiley's Advances in Civil Engineering.

I am proficient in both Bangla and English, and comfortable working on Windows and macOS environments.
